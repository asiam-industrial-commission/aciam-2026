Bienvenue à ACIAM 2026

Welcome address from the Chair and Co-Chair of ACIAM

Africa is inventing its future, fast, bold and resolute. ACIAM Burkina Faso 2026 celebrates mathematics as the spark that lights this transformation: the models that predict storms and protect communities, the algorithms that power factories and scale healthy systems, and the data-driven insights that unlock opportunity for millions. Here, ideas meet action, students, start-ups, researchers and policymakers will gather to imagine new possibilities and build partnerships that turn rigorous thinking into tangible progress. Join us to be part of a movement that harnesses mathematical creativity to strengthen communities, energize industry, and create a more resilient, equitable future for Africa and the world.
The African Conference for Industrial and Applied Mathematics (ACIAM) returns in October 2026 in Burkina Faso. Under the theme “Mathematics for Resilient Futures: Climate, Health and Industry”, ACIAM 2026 invites original contributions that harness mathematical theory, computation and data science to address pressing societal challenges.
